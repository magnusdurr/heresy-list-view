Stratagems

This is a collection of all stratagems for all factions, intended to give an overview of what is available. Stratagems are picked at the start of each battle, after deployment has been carried out, but before the first strategy roll. You may pick one stratagem if your army contains any allied formations. If your army contains no allied formations, you may pick two stratagems. All stratagems should be kept secret until the point they are used.

Legion Stratagems

Legiones Astartes players may pick stratagems from their own legion's, or from the Legiones Inductii category.

Legiones Inductii

Know No Fear

Once per battle, after a Legion formation has declared an action but before rolling to activate, you may declare the Know No Fear stratagem. The formation may immediately remove D3 BMs.

Dark Angels (I)
Decisive Fire

Once per battle, after a Dark Angels formation fails to activate when attempting a Sustain Fire action, you may declare the Decisive Fire stratagem to re-roll this action test. Remember that no roll can be re-rolled more than once.

Heat of Searing Light

Once per battle, after a Dark Angels formation targets an enemy formation with shooting, but before rolling to hit, you may declare the Heat of Searing Light stratagem. All attacks with the 'Energetic' special rule gains the 'Disrupt' and 'Crippling' special rules.

Emperor's Children (III)
Flawless Execution

Once per battle, after an Emperor's Children formation fails to activate when attempting a Double action, you may declare the Flawless Execution stratagem to re-roll this action test. Remember that no roll can be re-rolled more than once.

Perfect Counter

Once per battle, after an Emperor's Children formation has been Engaged by an enemy formation, you may declare the Perfect Counter stratagem. All units in that formation may do an additional 5 cm counter charge move.

Iron Warriors (IV)
Iron Within, Iron Without

Once per battle, after an Iron Warriors formation fails a Rally test, you may declare the Iron Within, Iron Without stratagem. You may re-roll this Rally test. Remember that no roll can be re-rolled more than once.

Bitter Fury

Once per battle, after an Iron Warriors formation has been caught in a Crossfire, you may declare the Bitter Fury stratagem. All units in that formation counts as having Thick Rear Armour until the end of the current action.

White Scars (V)
Chasing the Wind

Once per battle, after a White Scars formation fails to activate when attempting a March action, you may declare the Chasing the Wind stratagem. You may re-roll this action test with an additional +1 to the dice. Remember that no roll can be re-rolled more than once.

Born to the Saddle

Once per battle, after a White Scars formation has declared it will Double or March and has successfully activated, but before its action has been carried out, you may declare the Born in the Saddle stratagem. All units in that formation gains 5 cm extra speed for the entire action.

Space Wolves (VI)
No Prey Escapes the Wolf

Once per battle, before a Space Wolves formation fails to activate when attempting an Engage action, you may declare the No Prey Escapes the Wolf stratagem. You may re-roll this action test if at least one unit will get into base contact with an enemy unit. Remember that no roll can be re-rolled more than once.

Wolves of Fenris

Once per battle, after a Space Wolves formation has declared an Engage and has successfully activated, the formation may re-roll an additional D6 missed CC attacks during the assault. Remember that no die may be re-rolled more than once.

Imperial Fists (VII)
Dicipline and Resolve

Once per battle, after an Imperial Fists formation has declared it will Marshal, and has successfully activated, you may declare the Dicipline and Resolve stratagem. The formation must remain stationary, but may re-roll the dice when removing Blast Markers (the second result stands, even if worse than the first), and does not suffer the usual -1 to hit modifier for shooting.

Brilliant Defense

Once per battle, after an Imperial Fists formation that is occupying a Stronghold or Bastion has been declared the target of enemy Shooting, you may declare the Brilliant Defense stratagem. The Stronghold is immune to critical hits for the duration of the current action.

Night Lords (VIII)
Children of the Night

Once per battle, after a Night Lords formation has been declared the target of enemy shooting, but before any rolls to hit, you may declare the Children of the Night stratagem. All units in the formation counts as being in Cover until the end of the current action.

Better Part of Valour

Once per battle, after a Night Lords formation has been declared the target of enemy Shooting, you may declare the Better Part of Valour stratagem. After the current action is resolved, you may make a withdrawal move exactly as if the formation had become broken, but moving only once instead of twice.

Blood Angels (IX)
Thrall of the Red Thirst

Once per battle, after a Blood Angels formation has declared an Engage and has successfully activated, but before its action has been carried out, you may declare the Thrall of the Red Thirst stratagem. All units in the formation gain the 'Blitz' special rule until the end of the current action.

Children of Baal

Once per battle, after a Blood Angels formation targets an enemy formation with shooting, but before rolling to hit, you may declare the Children of Baal stratagem. All attacks with the 'MW' special rule gains the 'Disrupt' and 'Crippling' special rules.

Iron Hands (X)
Eyes of Vigilance

Once per battle, after an Iron Hands formation has successfully gone into Overwatch, you may declare the Eyes of Vigilance stratagem. All units in the formation gain an additional +1 modifier to hit if the Overwatch is used during the current turn.

Gorgon’s Spite

Once per battle, after an Iron Hands formation targets an enemy formation with shooting, but before rolling to hit, you may declare the Gorgon’s Spite stratagem. All attacks with the 'Graviton(x)' special rule gains the 'Titan Hunter' and 'Crippling' special rules.

World Eaters (XII)
Cloaked in Blood

Once per battle, after a World Eaters formation has successfully activated using an Engage action, you may declare the Cloaked in Blood stratagem. All units in the formation gains the 'Invulnerable Save' special rule for the duration of this action.

Violence Incarnate

Once per battle, after a World Eaters formation has declared it will Engage but before rolling to activate, you may declare the Violence Incarnate stratagem. The formation automatically succeeds with the activation with no roll required.

Ultramarines (XIII)
Unity of Purpose

Once per battle, after a Ultramarines formation has successfully retained the Initiative, but before its action has been carried out, you may declare the Unity of Purpose stratagem. Another Ultramarines formation within 15 cm of the first must attempt to retain the initiative again after the current action has been resolved, and must attempt to conduct the same action as the previous formation.

Aegis of Wisdom

Once per battle, after a Ultramarines formation has been declared the target of an enemy engage, but before the assault has been carried out, you may declare the Aegis of Wisdom stratagem. All units in the formation gains the 'Invulnerable Save' special rule for the duration of this action.

Death Guard (XIV)
Remorseless Advance

Once per battle, after a Death Guard formation has successfully activated, but before its action has been carried out, you may declare the Remorseless Advance stratagem. If the roll to activate is successful, the formation may re-roll all failed dangerous and difficult terrain tests until the end of the current action. Remember that no die may be re-rolled more than once.

Creeping Death

Once per battle, after a Death Guard formation targets an enemy formation with shooting, but before rolling to hit, you may declare the Creeping Death stratagem. At the end of the current action, but before checking if the enemy became broken, the target formation suffers an additional D6 BMs.

Thousand Sons (XV)
Fortress of the Mind

Once per battle, after a Thousand Sons formation has been declared the target of enemy shooting, but before any rolls to hit, you may declare the Fortress of the Mind stratagem. The formation will suffers D6 less BMs from the current action.

Secrets of Æther

Once per battle, after a Thousand Sons formation targets an enemy formation with shooting, but before rolling to hit, you may declare the Secrets of Æther stratagem. All rolled hits with the 'Æther-Fire' special rule gains the 'Disrupt' and 'Crippling' special rules.

Sons of Horus (XVI)
Chosen by the Dark Gods

Once per battle, after a Sons of Horus formation has declared an Engage and successfully activated, but before its action has been carried out, you may declare the Chosen by the Dark Gods stratagem. The formation counts as having the 'Daemonic' and 'Invulnerable Save' special rules until the end of the current turn.

Dark Emissary

Once per battle, after a Sons of Horus formation have been declared the target of an enemy Engage, but before the action has been carried out, you may declare the Dark Emissary stratagem. One unit with a Character in the formation gains the 'Inspiring', 'Invulnerable Save', and 'Psyker - Anathemata' special rules until the end of the current action.

Word Bearers (XVII)
True Believers

Once per battle, after a Word Bearers formation has become broken for any reason and has conducted their withdrawal move, you may declare the True Believers stratagem. The formation immediately rallies with no dice roll required.

Disciples Of Lorgar

Once per battle, at the end of an action where a Word Bearers formation wiped out an enemy formation with shooting or an assault, you may declare the Disciples Of Lorgar stratagem. All friendly Word Bearers formations may immediately remove d3 BMs. This has no effect on broken formations.

Salamanders (XVIII)
Promethean Will

Once per battle, after a Salamanders formation has lost an assault, you may declare the Promethean Will stratagem. The formation suffers no extra damage from hackdowns.

Redemption of Flames

Once per battle, after a Salamanders formation targets an enemy formation with shooting, but before rolling to hit, you may declare the Redemption of Flames stratagem. All rolled hits with the 'Ignore Cover' special rule gains the 'Disrupt' and 'Crippling' special rules.

Raven Guard (XIX)
Hidden Hand

Once per battle, after a Raven Guard formation has won an assault, you may declare the Hidden Hand stratagem. The formation may consolidate up to their full movement, instead of the usual 5 cm.

Fade to Black

Once per battle, after a Raven Guard formation fails an activation and takes a 'Hold' action, you may declare the Fade to Black stratagem. After the current action is resolved, you may make a withdrawal move exactly as if the formation had become broken, but moving only once instead of twice.

Alpha Legion (XX)
Sons Of The Hydra

Once per battle, after unit with an Alpha Legion Supreme Commander has been destroyed, you may declare the I am Alpharius stratagem. Replace a random unit with a Lieutenant character in a friendly Alpha Legion formation with the newly destroyed unit. If there are no Lieutenants in the army, the stratagem cannot be declared.

Mobius Configuration

Once per battle, after an opponent has declared a stratagem of any kind, you may declare the Mobius Configuration stratagem. You may immediately pick one of your opponents stratagems, or one of the Legiones Inductii stratagems, and use it at a later point in the battle.
